Welcome to PopHub!

PopHub is the place for finding interesting things on GitHub.

What is PopHub?

PopHub is a free service for finding interesting things on GitHub, for example interesting people according to followers, repositories or some other factor.

You can choose to follow people or not. You do not need an account to see all those interesting things.

Start by signing in through your GitHub-account. And you can start follow all those interesting people!
